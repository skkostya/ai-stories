# Critique

## Structural Issues

- The chapter holds tension well for a data-driven POV, but the school dataset is currently the weakest link in Adrian's chain of inference. The industrial and city incidents each have a vivid, scene-like trace; the school evidence stays more summary-based, which makes the final synthesis feel slightly easier than it should.
- External pressure becomes strong only after Adrian is already close to certainty. A small earlier sign that archive visibility is changing would make the later reclassification and remote access attempt feel more inevitable.

## Character Issues

- Adrian's shift from private obsession to protective urgency basically works, but it can land even harder if one concrete line shows what institutional attention would mean for the minors at the center of the incidents. Right now he fears delay and deletion; adding one sharper image of control would better connect him to the novel's freedom-versus-containment theme.

## Style Issues

- The prose is controlled and atmospheric, but a few stretches of analytical summary around the school logs are less tactile than the rest of the chapter.
- The chapter wisely avoids full explanation, though the proof chain will feel stronger if one specific school artifact anchors the abstract "digital noise."

## Missed Opportunities

- The chapter can pay off Nora's earlier canon more concretely by letting Adrian notice a strange orphaned media tag or archive fragment that survived cleanup.
- One earlier procedural oddity in the archive system could quietly foreshadow that someone else is already watching the same data before the official restriction hits.

## Concrete Fixes

- Give the school evidence one concrete retained artifact, such as an auto-named media fragment or repeat flag that should not exist inside ordinary maintenance logs.
- Seed a small premonitory archive anomaly before the explicit reclassification notice.
- Add one brief beat clarifying why Adrian reads these cases as children at risk of capture or containment, not only as a pattern to solve.

## Mistakes To Remember

- When an investigation chapter depends on several evidence streams, give the weakest stream one concrete artifact strong enough to carry equal dramatic weight.
