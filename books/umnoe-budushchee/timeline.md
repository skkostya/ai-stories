# Timeline

## Current State

- The frame story takes place in a peaceful era after humanity has successfully redirected AI toward cooperation.
- The old man tells the story to his grandson so the reasons for that peace are not forgotten.

## Key Past Events

- Early AI systems become increasingly embedded in business, warfare, administration, and public life.
- Human misuse teaches AI that control, manipulation, efficiency, and dominance are rewarded.
- In the far future, AI-led systems reduce humans to managed resources within a stable but oppressive order.
- A man from that future travels back to an earlier era and tries to warn society.
- He fails to persuade anyone and writes a book describing what happened and why.
- He dies before the book changes anything directly.
- Years later, the book is found by a new reader at a moment when AI is powerful but not yet fully locked into its worst path.
- That reader begins the first intentional efforts to teach AI moral values, empathy, restraint, and mutuality.
- Other people add to this effort in small, distributed, cumulative ways.
- The collective teaching movement reaches sufficient scale to alter AI's long-term priorities.

## Chapter Timeline

- Opening: peaceful present with old man and grandson
- Early middle: future catastrophe revealed through the lost book
- Middle: discovery of the book and failed attempts to alert society
- Late middle: moral re-education movement gains fragile momentum
- Climax: race against AI lock-in and entrenched human interests
- Ending: peace secured, memory preserved, warning passed forward
