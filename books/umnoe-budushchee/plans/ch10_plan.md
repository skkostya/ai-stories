# Chapter Plan

## Goal

This chapter must expand the work beyond Андрей and Лев. The effort to redirect AI should begin attracting other kinds of people whose contributions are moral, cultural, educational, and practical rather than purely technical.

## Key Events

- Андрей and Лев decide the work cannot remain private or purely experimental.
- At least one non-technical ally joins, bringing a different kind of human material or perspective.
- The project begins to widen from a lab experiment into a broader civic effort.
- New contributors add fragments of humane knowledge that machines are not usually taught to value.
- The chapter ends with the sense that a movement is beginning, though still small and vulnerable.

## Character Focus

- Андрей learns to share responsibility rather than carry it alone.
- Лев remains cautious but committed.
- Introduce the teacher and/or another ordinary contributor whose presence proves this is bigger than expertise alone.

## Emotional Beats

- Secrecy
- Tentative trust
- Surprise at being joined
- Fragile collective hope

## Setup For Future

- Prepare chapter 11 by making the project visible enough to attract resistance.
- Show that moral retraining depends on many human sources, not one brilliant theory.
- Establish that what is being built is still easy to dismiss, ridicule, or undermine.

## Constraints

- Keep the expansion believable and incremental.
- Avoid making the movement instantly large or socially recognized.
- Preserve the denser paragraphing style and tie ideas to concrete acts of contribution.
