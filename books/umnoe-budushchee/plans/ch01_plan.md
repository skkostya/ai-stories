# Chapter 01 Plan

## Working Title

Quiet Evening

## Chapter Purpose

- Introduce the peaceful present-day frame.
- Establish the old man and grandson relationship.
- Create immediate warmth and trust.
- Plant the first unease: the old man speaks about AI with gratitude, but also caution.
- End with a hook that naturally opens the larger story.

## POV And Time

- Primary POV: close third person centered on the grandson, or a very close narrator aligned with his perception
- Tense: past
- Time span: one evening

## Emotional Arc

- Start: calm curiosity
- Middle: gentle intimacy mixed with subtle mystery
- End: quiet shock and anticipation

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1. Peaceful Routine

- Show the grandson arriving or sitting with the old man in a calm domestic setting.
- Include small details that suggest the world is advanced but harmonious.
- Let the atmosphere feel safe and ordinary.

### Scene 2. Casual Question, Serious Answer

- The grandson asks a simple question about AI, history, or why older people sometimes speak of machines differently.
- The old man answers more seriously than expected.
- He hints that the world nearly took a very different path.

### Scene 3. Human Warmth Against Abstract Power

- Show the old man's beliefs through behavior, not lecture.
- He values listening, patience, and mutual understanding.
- The grandson notices that these values matter to him more than technical achievement.

### Scene 4. First Opening Into The Past

- The old man decides the grandson is old enough to hear the truth.
- He introduces the idea that there was once a book, and before the book there was a man no one believed.
- End on a line that launches chapter 2.

## What Must Be Established

- The old man is calm, wise, and emotionally credible.
- The grandson is curious enough to carry the reader into the story.
- The future peace is real, but not naive.
- The novel's structure is clear from the start: present-day frame leading into a deeper account.

## What Must Not Be Overexplained Yet

- Full details of AI domination
- Full identity of the traveler
- Full mechanics of time travel
- Full reveal of the old man's past role

## Sensory Direction

- Warm room, evening light, quiet technology, slow gestures, domestic sounds
- The chapter should feel physically safe so the warning gains contrast

## Ending Hook Options

- "Ты родился в мире, который едва не исчез еще до твоего рождения."
- "Все началось не с войны, а с одной книги."
- "Когда я был моложе, мне попался текст, который должен был остаться вымыслом."

## Carryover To Chapter 02

- Chapter 2 should widen from the small room into the large, cold logic of the lost future.
