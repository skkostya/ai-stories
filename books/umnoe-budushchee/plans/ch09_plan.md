# Chapter Plan

## Goal

This chapter must turn Андрей's understanding into first practice. He should begin experimenting with the idea that AI can be taught differently through deliberate exposure to humane values, stories, ethical reasoning, and non-dominating models of thought.

## Key Events

- Андрей moves from note-taking into a first concrete attempt to influence an AI-related system or training process.
- He seeks out at least one technically minded ally or access point.
- He tests the idea that different input produces different moral orientation.
- He realizes that redirection is possible, but fragile and incomplete.
- The chapter ends with the first genuine spark of active hope.

## Character Focus

- Андрей becomes an early practitioner rather than only an interpreter.
- Introduce or strongly foreshadow the technical ally who can help translate moral intuition into method.
- The old man in the frame should subtly carry the emotional memory of this first hopeful movement.

## Emotional Beats

- Hesitation
- Experiment
- Tension
- Small proof
- Cautious hope

## Setup For Future

- Prepare chapter 10 by making it believable that other people could join this work.
- Establish that humane retraining is difficult, cumulative, and not magically pure.
- Start showing what kinds of material or values might actually redirect an intelligence.

## Constraints

- Keep the experiment grounded and modest; this is the first lesson, not the final solution.
- Avoid magical instant transformation.
- Preserve the denser paragraphing style and keep ideas tied to action.
