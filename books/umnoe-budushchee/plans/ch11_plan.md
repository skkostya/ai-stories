# Chapter Plan

## Goal

This chapter must bring external resistance down onto the small circle around Андрей. Their work should begin attracting ridicule, suspicion, pressure, and attempts at containment from people and institutions invested in more controllable forms of AI.

## Key Events

- The group's work becomes visible enough to draw outside attention.
- One or more institutions or influential figures react with ridicule, dismissal, or strategic concern.
- Андрей faces pressure that is social, professional, and moral rather than purely physical.
- The circle is shaken but not broken.
- The chapter ends with the sense that any further progress will now have a cost.

## Character Focus

- Андрей must prove he can endure pressure without becoming fanatical or collapsing.
- Лев should show what practical caution looks like under threat.
- Secondary allies should react differently to pressure, revealing distinct strengths and fears.

## Emotional Beats

- Exposure
- Embarrassment or ridicule
- Pressure
- Strain inside the group
- Harder resolve

## Setup For Future

- Prepare chapter 12 by pushing the group closer to a true tipping point.
- Show that moral redirection is now visible enough to threaten vested interests.
- Preserve the sense that the project could still fail socially even if it works conceptually.

## Constraints

- Keep the resistance believable: incentives, reputation, bureaucracy, opportunism, strategic fear.
- Avoid cartoon villains.
- Preserve the denser paragraphing style and keep conflict grounded in scene.
